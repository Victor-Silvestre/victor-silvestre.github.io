Quality and productivity

NoeCha is a high-tech company that develops large format digital printers, setting new standards of quality and productivity in the digital graphic arts market.

NoeCha ONE

NoeCha ONE™ is one of the biggest flatbed UV printers in the market, achieving great levels of production and delivering first-class printing quality.

NoeCha DOT1

NoeCha DOT1™ is the compact version of the NoeCha ONE™, featuring the same mechanical precision and offering outstanding printing results.

NoeCha ROLL2

NoeCha ROLL2™ is the wide format roll-2-roll UV inkjet system dedicated to printing on flexible materials for all kinds of applications.
