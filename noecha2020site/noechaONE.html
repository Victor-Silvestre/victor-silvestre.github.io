NoeCha ONE

Amazing quality at production speed.

The Large Format Industrial UV LED Flatbed

Characterized by outstanding quality and high productivity, NoeCha ONE™ is the perfect printer to produce high impact graphics, POS display as well as particular industrial applications.

Make the most of digital printing

  • Inkjet printer
  • UV-LED cool curing
  • Print up to 487 sqm/h
  • Photo-realistic quality
  • Low operational cost
  • Sectioned 3.2 x 2 meters bed
  • Upgradable

UV printing technology. Instant results

The UV-LED cool curing system allows excellent printability on any type of material, including temperature-sensitive ones.

The use of the LED guarantees very low operational cost and energy savings.

The printer that grows with your business

NoeCha ONE is a modular system. It is designed to operate up to 8 inks with up to 5 print heads per channel.

The system is always upgradable from one configuration to another, protecting the initial investment.

Perfectly adapted to your dayly production

The 3.2 x 2 meters 16-zoned vacuum table of NoeCha ONE covers all media standard sizes. If that is not enough, we can design the vacuum zones especially to suit your needs.

The autofeeding system is also sectioned, which makes possible to load/unload automatically, and print up to 6 separated substrates at the same time.

Print fine detail at top speed

Any kind of rigid or semi-rigid material up to 100mm thickness. Quality prints effectively piled on the unload platform, ready to deliver.

Technical specifications

Technology: Piezo Drop-on-demand Inkjet technology.

Ink Colors: Four colours (CMYK) with the availability to include white (before and after), varnish or spot colors (orange, violet and green).

Resolution: Native 600x600 dots per inch in single pass. 4 grayscale levels from 3 to 14 picolitres.

Printing area: 3200 x 2000 millimetres 16-zoned sectioned vacuum table.

Ink system: UV-LED curable inks stored in 12-litre capacity tanks.

Media type: Availability to print on any type of rigid and semi-rigid media; PVC, foam, cardboard, glass, wood, aluminum, metal, etc.

Maximum weight: 80 kg/sqm.

Maximum size: 3200 x 2050 millimetres.

Media handling: It is possible to load the media both manually and using the automatic feeder and stacker system.

Thickness: From 0,1 to 100 millimetres.

NoeCha ONE™ - 20k: From 65 up to 221 sqm/h.

NoeCha ONE™ - 30k: From 91 up to 304 sqm/h.

NoeCha ONE™ - 40k: From 120 up to 406 sqm/h.

NoeCha ONE™ - 50k: From 140 up to 487 sqm/h.

Printer dimensions: 8300 mm Width x 4760 mm Length x 2240 mm Height.

Printer Weight: 13500 kg.

Operating interface: Touch screen operating software based on Windows 10 embedded.

RIP software: The printer can operate with various RIP software. However, Grand RIP+ by Caldera included is the only one recommended.
